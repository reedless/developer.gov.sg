SPOTON

What is SPOTON?

SPOTON is a smart thermal scanner developed under the Digital Operations Smart Services (DOSS) platform. SPOTON can screen up to ten people at once, with a temperature indicator for each face and automated alarms and email alerts when high temperatures are detected.

With SPOTON, fast crowd temperature screening is enabled by detecting and alerting operators to individuals with high temperatures. This minimises physical contact and time spent on manual temperature checks.

As a hybrid AI solution, it combines affordable, commercial off-the-shelf hardware (a long-wave infrared camera and RGB-Depth camera) with advanced software capabilities like deep learning human face detection. This combination makes SPOTON significantly more affordable than most commercially available scanners without compromising on accuracy.

Measuring just 90mm x 85mm x 30mm, SPOTON is a lightweight mobile device that can be easily deployed both indoors and outdoors with a simple calibration process.

What are the features available?

  • Human face detection – SPOTON recognises only humans, not objects. It is not affected by masks, hats and headdresses, or by hot and cold objects (such as drinks).

  • Colour-coded temperature indicators per face – SPOTON displays the temperature detected on a colour-coded bounding box surrounding each face: Green for temperatures under 37.5°C and red for temperatures above 37.5°C.

  • Detects up to 10 faces at once

  • Alarm and email alerts – When SPOTON detects a temperature above 37.5°C, it triggers an alarm and email alerts to operators. Snapshots of high temperatures detected are automatically saved into a local folder.

  • ±0.3-0.5°C accuracy within a 1.5-2m range*

*In calibration tests conducted with the National Metrology Centre (NMC), SPOTON demonstrated an accuracy of ±0.28°C–0.33 °C for temperature ranges of 36°C, 37°C and 38°C, with NMC’s recommended pixel count of 10(V) by 10(H) at a distance of 1.65m.

What are some use cases of SPOTON?

  • SPOTON comes in handy for any environment with high footfall. It helps you save time and labour on temperature screening while keeping your operators and visitors safe.

  • SPOTON has been tested and deployed in different settings, including office buildings, community centres, places of worship, retail spaces and community isolation facilities.

  • SPOTON can be deployed at any indoor or outdoor environment (away from direct sunlight and with a stable ambient temperature).

What are some upcoming plans for SPOTON?

For SPOTON v2.0, we are developing API extensibility for seamless integration with other IoT or robotic systems. We are also working on developing more deep-learning algorithms to expand SPOTON’s capabilities beyond thermal scanning.

How can I get started?

As countries continue their efforts in tackling COVID-19, we hope to make the SPOTON software available to the industry and tech community in the future. To work with us, contact sensors@tech.gov.sg.
